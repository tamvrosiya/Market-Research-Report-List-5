<p><p>Hot-pressed magnets are a type of neodymium iron boron magnet that are manufactured using a hot pressing process. There are two main types of hot-pressed magnets: isotropic and anisotropic. Isotropic magnets have equal magnetic properties in all directions, making them ideal for applications where magnet orientation is not crucial. Anisotropic magnets, on the other hand, are magnetized in a specific direction during the hot extrusion process, resulting in higher magnetic strength in that direction. Both types of hot-pressed magnets are widely used in various industries for their high magnetic properties and performance.</p></p>
