<p><p>Single seat rowboats can be classified into two main market types: sculling rowboats and sweep rowboats. Sculling rowboats are designed for rowing with two oars, one in each hand, allowing for more independent and versatile rowing. On the other hand, sweep rowboats are designed for rowing with one oar, requiring coordination with other rowers in a team. Both types of rowboats cater to different preferences and rowing styles, offering options for solo rowers or those who enjoy rowing as part of a team.</p></p>
