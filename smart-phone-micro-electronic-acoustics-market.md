Speakers are essential for audio playback, providing sound output for calls and multimedia experiences. Receivers, typically found in earphones or devices, convert electrical signals back into audible sound for private listening. Together, these elements enhance communication and entertainment experiences, driving innovations in sound quality and miniaturization in smartphones.</p></p>
