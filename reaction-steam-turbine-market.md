<p><strong>The Reaction Steam Turbine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Flow</li><li>Radial Flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reaction steam turbines can be classified based on the flow of steam through the turbine blades. Axial flow turbines have blades that are aligned parallel to the axis of rotation, allowing steam to flow in the same direction as the rotor. Radial flow turbines, on the other hand, have blades that direct steam outward from the center of the turbine before exiting tangentially. Each type of turbine offers unique advantages and is suited for different applications based on factors such as efficiency, power output, and space constraints.</p></p>
