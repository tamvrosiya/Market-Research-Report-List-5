<p><ul><li>Liquid saturated polyester resin</li><li>Solid saturated polyester resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Saturated polyester resin is available in two main types: liquid and solid forms. Liquid saturated polyester resin is a viscous liquid that is commonly used in coatings, adhesives, and inks. It offers excellent flow properties and can be easily applied to various substrates. On the other hand, solid saturated polyester resin is a solid material that is often used in powder coatings and molding compounds. It provides high mechanical strength and excellent heat resistance. Both forms of saturated polyester resin offer unique properties and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>
