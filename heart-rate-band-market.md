<p><p>Heart Rate Bands are available in various types depending on their frequency bands and transmission methods. Low Frequency bands operate at 5.3 kHz and are suitable for basic heart rate monitoring. High Frequency bands operate at 2.4 GHz and offer more advanced features such as fitness tracking and smartphone connectivity. ANT Heart Rate Bands use ANT technology for wireless communication. Bluetooth Heart Rate Bands connect to smartphones or other devices via Bluetooth. Coded Transmission uses coded signals for more secure data transmission, while Uncoded Transmission sends data without encoding.</p></p>
