<p><p>RFID in Pharmaceuticals Market includes three main types: RFID Readers, RFID Tags, and RFID Middleware. RFID Readers are devices used to read and transmit data from RFID tags. RFID Tags are small electronic devices that store and transmit data wirelessly. RFID Middleware is software that manages communication between RFID readers and enterprise systems, helping to streamline data collection and integration. All three components work together to improve inventory management, track products, and enhance supply chain visibility in the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
