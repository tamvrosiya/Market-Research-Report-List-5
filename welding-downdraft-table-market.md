<p><p>A welding downdraft table is a specialized piece of equipment used in welding operations to remove fumes, smoke, and dust from the air. It typically consists of a large tabletop with grates or holes through which air is drawn down, capturing the contaminants and pulling them into a filtration system. This helps to create a safer and more comfortable working environment for welders by reducing the risk of exposure to harmful airborne particles.</p><p>The welding downdraft table market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.7% during the forecast period.
