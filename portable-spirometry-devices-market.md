<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Portable Spirometry Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flow-Sensing Spirometry Devices</li><li>Peak Flow Spirometry Devices</li><li>Volume Measurement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable spirometry devices are classified into three main types based on their functionality: Flow-Sensing Spirometry Devices, Peak Flow Spirometry Devices, and Volume Measurement devices. Flow-Sensing devices measure the rate of airflow during inhalation and exhalation, providing valuable information about lung function. Peak Flow devices measure the maximum airflow that can be generated during forced exhalation, helping to assess the severity of conditions like asthma. Volume Measurement devices calculate the total volume of air that moves in and out of the lungs during breathing, aiding in diagnosing respiratory disorders.</p></p>
