<p><p>The erythritol market primarily consists of two main types: erythritol powder and erythritol granular. Erythritol powder features a fine, crystalline texture that dissolves quickly, making it ideal for baking and beverages. In contrast, erythritol granular has a coarser texture, resembling traditional sugar, and is commonly used in various food products for bulk sweetening. Both forms are popular for their low-calorie content and natural sweetness, catering to the rising demand for healthier sugar alternatives in the food and beverage industry.</p></p>
