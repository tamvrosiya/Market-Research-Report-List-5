Hemodialysis Membranes are used in dialysis machines to remove waste and excess fluid from the blood. Leukocyte Filters are designed to remove white blood cells from donated blood to reduce the risk of transfusion reactions. Plasma Separation Membranes are used to separate plasma from blood cells for therapeutic purposes. Each type plays a crucial role in maintaining the health and well-being of patients with various medical conditions.</p></p>
