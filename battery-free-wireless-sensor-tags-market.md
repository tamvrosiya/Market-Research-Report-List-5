<p><p>Battery-free wireless sensor tags come in three main market types: active tags, passive tags, and semi-active tags. </p><p>Active tags have their own power source and can continuously transmit data over long distances. Passive tags do not have their own power source and are activated by an external reader, making them less expensive but with shorter range. Semi-active tags have a battery for onboard processing and can be activated by an external reader or through sensor-triggered events. Each type offers different benefits and is suitable for various applications.</p></p>
