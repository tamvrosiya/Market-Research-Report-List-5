<p><p>The protein chip market encompasses various types, including reverse phase protein microarrays, functional protein microarrays, and analytical microarrays. Reverse phase protein microarrays focus on detecting and quantifying proteins from complex samples, often used in biomarker discovery. Functional protein microarrays assess the activity and interactions of proteins under specific conditions, aiding in drug development. Analytical microarrays combine multiple assays for simultaneous analysis, providing comprehensive data on protein profiles. Together, these technologies enhance research across proteomics and personalized medicine.</p></p>
