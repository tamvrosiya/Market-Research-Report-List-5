<p><ul><li>Wrap Around Style Tubing Hanger</li><li>Mandrel Type Tubing Hanger</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tubing hangers are used in oil and gas wells to support and seal tubing strings. The market types include wrap-around style tubing hangers, which wrap around the tubing and provide support. Mandrel type tubing hangers are installed inside the tubing string with a mandrel for support. Other types of tubing hangers include slip type tubing hangers, threaded tubing hangers, and latch type tubing hangers. Each type has its own advantages and is used in different well conditions to ensure proper support and sealing of the tubing strings.</p></p>
