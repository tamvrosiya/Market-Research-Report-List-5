<p><ul><li>Active Soil Depressurization (Interior)</li><li>Active Soil Depressurization (Exterior)</li><li>Active Soil Depressurization (Slab)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Whole-House Radon Mitigation Systems are available in three main types: Active Soil Depressurization (Interior), Active Soil Depressurization (Exterior), and Active Soil Depressurization (Slab). The Interior system involves a fan installed in the basement or crawl space to create negative pressure, while the Exterior system is placed outside the home to draw radon out before it enters. The Slab system is designed for homes with a concrete slab foundation, with a pipe system installed beneath the slab to vent radon gas.</p></p>
