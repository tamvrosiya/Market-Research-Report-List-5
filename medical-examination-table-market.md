<p><p>Medical examination tables come in different types based on their operation mechanism. Manual tables require physical effort to adjust height and position. Hydraulic tables use fluid pressure to raise and lower the table. Electric tables are operated using electricity for smooth and effortless adjustments. Pneumatic tables use air pressure for height adjustments, providing a more controlled and precise positioning. Each type caters to different needs and preferences, offering a range of options for healthcare professionals to choose from.</p></p>
