<p><p>The radar detector market comprises several types catering to different consumer needs. Full-band radar detectors can detect all frequency bands used by law enforcement, offering comprehensive protection. GPS radar detectors integrate GPS technology to recognize fixed speed cameras and alert users accordingly. GPS full-band radar detectors combine the features of both, providing extensive band detection along with GPS capabilities. Ordinary radar detectors, while effective, typically lack advanced features like GPS, making them a basic option for users who prioritize simplicity.</p></p>
