<p><p>GNSS SoCs market can be classified into various types based on the frequency bands they operate on - L1 Band, B1 Band, E1 Band, L5 Band, and Others. L1 Band is commonly used for GPS signals, B1 Band for GLONASS, E1 Band for Galileo, and L5 Band for GPS modernization. The Others category includes additional frequency bands or combinations. Each type serves specific navigation and timing applications, catering to diverse needs in the GNSS technology market.</p></p>
