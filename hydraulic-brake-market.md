<p><p>The hydraulic brake market primarily comprises hydraulic disc brakes, hydraulic drum brakes, and other specialized braking systems. Hydraulic disc brakes utilize fluid pressure to clamp brake pads against a rotor, providing superior stopping power and heat dissipation, making them popular in high-performance vehicles. Hydraulic drum brakes, on the other hand, feature a drum that houses the brake shoes, which expand outward to create friction. Other systems may include advanced technologies like anti-lock braking systems (ABS) and regenerative braking, enhancing safety and efficiency.</p></p>
