<p><p>Silicon IP (Intellectual Property) refers to pre-designed, pre-verified reusable blocks of logic or circuitry that are used in the design of integrated circuits (ICs). By incorporating silicon IP into their designs, semiconductor companies can reduce development time and cost, while also increasing the overall efficiency and performance of their products.</p><p>The Silicon IP market is experiencing rapid growth, driven by the increasing complexity of IC designs and the growing demand for advanced electronic devices.
