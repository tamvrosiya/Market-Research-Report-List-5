<p><p>Downstream bioprocessing market includes various types such as downstream purification, buffer preparation and supply, process analytical testing, bulk storage and final fill. Downstream purification involves separating and purifying the desired product from the fermentation broth. Buffer preparation and supply involves providing the necessary buffers for various stages of the process. Process analytical testing includes monitoring and analyzing the process parameters in real-time. Bulk storage is the storage of the purified product before final fill, which is the process of filling the product into containers for distribution.</p></p>
