Monopolar grounding pads have one electrode and are commonly used in procedures where the current flows from a single point to the pad. Bipolar grounding pads have two electrodes and are used in procedures where the current flows between the two pads. Both types are essential for ensuring patient safety during surgeries and other medical interventions.</p></p>
