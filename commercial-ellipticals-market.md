<p><ul><li>Flywheel Front Ellipticals</li><li>Flywheel Rear Ellipticals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial ellipticals market is divided into two main types based on the position of the flywheel, which affects the feel and efficiency of the workout. Flywheel front ellipticals have the wheel positioned at the front, providing a smoother and more natural stride motion. On the other hand, flywheel rear ellipticals have the wheel placed at the back, offering a more challenging workout by requiring more effort from the user to maintain balance and stability. Both types cater to different preferences and fitness goals.</p></p>
