<p><p>Portable Interactive Whiteboard market consists of two main types: Front Projection and Rear Projection. Front Projection whiteboards are designed to project images from the front onto the whiteboard surface, which allows for easy visibility and interaction. Rear Projection whiteboards, on the other hand, project images from behind the whiteboard surface, providing a clearer display and eliminating shadow interference. Both types offer versatility and convenience for presentations, meetings, and educational purposes.</p></p>
