<p><p>The syngas market encompasses multiple production methods. Natural gas reforming involves converting methane into syngas using steam and catalysts, often favored for its efficiency. Naphtha reforming utilizes liquid hydrocarbons to produce syngas and is suitable for regions with abundant naphtha. Coal gasification transforms solid coal into syngas through high-temperature processes, providing a key resource in coal-rich areas. Other markets include biomass and waste gasification, which emphasize sustainability by converting organic materials into syngas, expanding energy production options.</p></p>
