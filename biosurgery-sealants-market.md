<p><p>The biosurgery sealants market includes fibrin sealants and collagen sealants, each serving unique applications in surgical procedures. Fibrin sealants mimic the final stages of blood coagulation, providing superior adhesion and hemostasis, often used in vascular and orthopedic surgeries. Collagen sealants, derived from natural collagen, enhance tissue bonding and promote healing, making them suitable for use in soft tissue repairs and cosmetic procedures. Both types are essential for improving surgical outcomes and reducing complications in various medical settings.</p></p>
