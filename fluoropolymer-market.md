<p><ul><li>Solid</li><li>Solution</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluoropolymer market can be categorized into solid and solution forms. Solid fluoropolymers are typically in the form of granules, powders, or pellets, and are used for manufacturing extruded films, sheets, and molded products. On the other hand, solution fluoropolymers are dissolved in a solvent and are used for applications such as coatings, adhesives, and sealants. Both solid and solution fluoropolymers offer excellent chemical and heat resistance, making them ideal for a wide range of industries including automotive, electronics, and aerospace.</p></p>
