<p><p>AC Induction Motors are a type of electric motor that converts electrical energy into mechanical energy using alternating current (AC). These motors are widely used in various applications, including industrial machinery, HVAC systems, and household appliances, due to their robustness and efficiency.</p><p>The AC Induction Motors Market is positioned for significant growth, expected to expand at a CAGR of 10.70% during the forecast period.
