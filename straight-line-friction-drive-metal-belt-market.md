<p><p>Straight Line Friction Drive Metal Belt markets can be categorized into two main types - Solid and Perforated. </p><p>Solid metal belts are flat, smooth belts without any gaps or holes. They provide a continuous surface for the drive system to operate on, offering consistent and reliable performance. </p><p>On the other hand, Perforated metal belts feature regularly spaced holes throughout the belt surface. These holes allow for airflow and drainage, making them ideal for applications where liquids or gases need to pass through the belt.</p></p>
