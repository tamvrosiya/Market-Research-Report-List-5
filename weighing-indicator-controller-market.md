<p><p>Weighing indicator controllers come in two main types: VFD display and LED display. VFD display controllers utilize Vacuum Fluorescent Display technology to provide clear and easy-to-read weight measurements. On the other hand, LED display controllers use Light Emitting Diodes to show weight information in a bright and energy-efficient manner. Both types offer different visual advantages and cater to different preferences in the market, providing options for customers looking for specific display features in their weighing indicator controllers.</p></p>
