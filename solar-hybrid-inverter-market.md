<p><p>Solar Hybrid Inverters combine the functions of a solar inverter and a battery inverter, allowing for both grid-tied and off-grid operation. Single-phase hybrid inverters are designed for residential and small commercial applications, while three-phase hybrid inverters are typically used in larger commercial and industrial setups. Single-phase inverters provide power to single-phase AC loads, while three-phase inverters support three-phase AC loads, providing flexibility and efficiency in meeting the varying energy needs of different types of consumers.</p></p>
