<p><p>The Bluetooth SoC market can be categorized into three main types: Single Mode, Dual-Mode, and Other. Single Mode SoCs support only Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), optimized for power efficiency and ideal for IoT devices. Dual-Mode SoCs can handle both classic Bluetooth and BLE, allowing versatility in communication with a wider range of devices. The "Other" category includes specialized or hybrid SoCs that may integrate additional wireless protocols, catering to niche applications or unique market demands.</p></p>
