<p><ul><li>Structural DNA Nanotechnology</li><li>Dynamic DNA Nanotechnology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Structural DNA nanotechnology involves the programmed assembly of DNA molecules to create nanoscale structures with specific shapes and sizes. These structures can be used for applications such as drug delivery, sensors, and molecular machines. On the other hand, dynamic DNA nanotechnology involves the design of DNA-based systems that can undergo controlled changes in response to environmental cues. This technology has potential applications in molecular robotics, nanoscale computing, and targeted drug delivery. Both types of DNA nanotechnology are rapidly advancing fields with promising implications for various industries.</p></p>
