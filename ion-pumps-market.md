<p><ul><li>Conventional/Standard Diode Pump</li><li>Noble Diode Ion Pump</li><li>Triode Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ion pumps are critical components in vacuum systems for maintaining low pressure by removing gas molecules. Conventional/standard diode pumps utilize a single anode and cathode for ionization. Noble diode ion pumps use noble gases to enhance pumping efficiency. Triode pumps feature two additional electrodes, allowing for better control of ion flow. Each type of ion pump offers unique benefits and is chosen based on specific application requirements for maintaining ultra-high vacuum levels in various scientific and industrial settings.</p></p>
