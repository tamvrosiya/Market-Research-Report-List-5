These trends are expected to further drive the market growth in the coming years.</p></p>

<p><p>The Artificial Heart Stent Market includes three main types: Bare-metal Stent (BMS), Drug-eluting Stent (DES), and Bioresorbable Vascular Scaffold (BVS). Bare-metal Stents are metal mesh tubes used to open narrowed arteries, while Drug-eluting Stents are coated with medication to prevent re-narrowing. Bioresorbable Vascular Scaffolds are designed to gradually dissolve in the body over time. Each type of stent has its own advantages and limitations, catering to the specific needs of patients with heart conditions.</p></p>
