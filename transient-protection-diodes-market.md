<p><p>Transient protection diodes are used to protect electronic circuits from voltage spikes and transients. Uni-polar transient protection diodes are designed to protect against voltage spikes in one direction only, while bi-polar transient protection diodes can protect against spikes in both directions. Uni-polar diodes are commonly used in applications where voltage spikes occur predominantly in one direction, such as power supplies. Bi-polar diodes are used in applications where voltage spikes can occur in both directions, such as telecommunications equipment and automotive electronics.</p></p>
