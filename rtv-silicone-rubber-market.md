<p><p>RTV Silicone Rubber is a versatile material used for a variety of applications. There are two main types of RTV Silicone Rubber markets: RTV-1 and RTV-2. RTV-1 refers to one-component systems that cure at room temperature when exposed to moisture in the air. These are typically used for sealing and bonding applications. RTV-2, on the other hand, refers to two-component systems that require mixing before curing. These are often used for mold making and casting applications where dimensional stability and high strength are required.</p></p>
