<p><p>Thermal Interface Materials (TIMs) facilitate efficient heat transfer between surfaces in electronic devices. Key types include thermal pads, which provide a solid interface; thermal grease and thermal paste, liquid substances that conform to surfaces for improved contact; thermal adhesives, which bond components while conducting heat; and gap fillers, designed to fill irregular spaces. Each type serves specific applications in cooling systems, ensuring optimal performance and longevity of electronic components by mitigating overheating and enhancing thermal management.</p></p>
