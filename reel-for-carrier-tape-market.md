<p><ul><li>Antistatic</li><li>Non-antistatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reel for carrier tape market is primarily segmented into antistatic and non-antistatic categories. Antistatic reels are designed to prevent static electricity buildup, making them essential for sensitive electronic components that can be damaged by electrostatic discharge. Non-antistatic reels, while generally less costly, are suitable for components that are less vulnerable to static. The choice between the two types depends on the specific application requirements in industries such as electronics, automotive, and telecommunications, where component protection is crucial.</p></p>
