<p><strong>The Blood Transport Boxes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Cooling Blood Transportation Box</li><li>Active Cooling Blood Transportation Box</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passive cooling blood transportation boxes use insulated materials to maintain a consistent temperature during transport, without the need for external power sources. They are often used for short distance transportation and have a lower cost compared to active cooling boxes. Active cooling blood transportation boxes, on the other hand, use built-in cooling systems such as refrigeration units or gel packs to actively regulate and maintain temperature during transport. These boxes are typically used for longer distance transportation or when strict temperature control is necessary.</p></p>
