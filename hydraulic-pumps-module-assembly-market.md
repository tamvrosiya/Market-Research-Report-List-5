<p><p>Hydraulic pumps module assembly market types include various components such as cylinder, eccentric wheel, plunger, spring, suction valve, and discharge valve. The cylinder is a crucial part responsible for transferring fluid power, while the eccentric wheel helps in converting rotary motion into linear motion. The plunger creates pressure within the pump, and the spring assists in controlling movements. The suction valve allows fluid into the pump, and the discharge valve directs the fluid out. All these components work together to ensure efficient hydraulic pump operation.</p></p>
