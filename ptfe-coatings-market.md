<p><p>PTFE coatings market includes solvent-based coatings and water-based coatings. Solvent-based coatings are formulated with high percentages of organic solvents and provide superior adhesion and durability. They are commonly used for industrial applications. On the other hand, water-based coatings are environmentally friendly, containing low levels of volatile organic compounds. They are preferred for their ease of application, quick drying time, and low toxicity. Both types of coatings offer excellent non-stick and heat resistance properties, making them popular choices in various industries.</p></p>
