<p><p>ESD diodes are used to protect electronic devices from damage caused by electrostatic discharge. There are different types of ESD diodes available in the market, including Zener diodes, Varistors, Silicon Controlled Rectifiers, Transient Voltage Suppressors, and others. Zener diodes regulate voltage and are commonly used in voltage regulation circuits. Varistors protect against voltage spikes. Silicon Controlled Rectifiers are used for power control applications. Transient Voltage Suppressors protect against voltage transients. Other types of ESD diodes offer specialized protections for specific applications.</p></p>
