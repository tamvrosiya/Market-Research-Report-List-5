<p><p>Bioprocess Integrity Testing Systems market includes Destructive Testing and Non-Destructive Testing types. Destructive Testing involves physically altering the sample to assess its structural integrity, which can include cutting, bending, or breaking the sample. Non-Destructive Testing, on the other hand, allows for evaluating the integrity of the sample without causing any damage. This can include methods such as ultrasound, radiography, or visual inspection. Both types of testing play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and reliability of bioprocess systems.</p></p>
