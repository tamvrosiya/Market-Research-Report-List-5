<p><p>Hydrogen gas onsite generators produce hydrogen gas through electrolysis. There are two main types of market technologies for these generators: traditional alkaline electrolyzers and PEM electrolyzers. Traditional alkaline electrolyzers use a liquid alkaline solution as an electrolyte, while PEM electrolyzers use a solid polymer membrane. Both technologies offer efficient and sustainable ways to produce hydrogen gas on-site for various industrial applications, such as fuel cells, energy storage, and transportation. These generators play a crucial role in advancing the green hydrogen economy.</p></p>
