<p><strong>The Tailgate Liner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drop-in Bedliners</li><li>Spray-on Bedliners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tailgate liner market consists of two main types: Drop-in Bedliners and Spray-on Bedliners. Drop-in Bedliners are typically made of molded plastic and can be easily removed for cleaning or replacement. They provide good protection against scratches and dents but may trap dirt and moisture underneath. Spray-on Bedliners are applied directly onto the truck bed, providing a custom fit and seamless protection against impact and corrosion. They are more durable and long-lasting compared to drop-in bedliners. Both options cater to different customer preferences and needs in the market.</p></p>
