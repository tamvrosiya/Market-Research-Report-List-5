<p><p>Automotive Laser Position Sensors come in two main types: Compact and Ultra-Compact. Compact sensors are slightly larger in size but offer higher accuracy and longer range measurement capabilities. They are ideal for applications where precision is crucial, such as autonomous driving systems. On the other hand, Ultra-Compact sensors are smaller in size, making them suitable for space-constrained environments like parking assistance systems. Despite their smaller size, Ultra-Compact sensors still offer reliable performance and accuracy in measuring distances in automotive applications.</p></p>
