<p><strong>The Ceramic Band Heaters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-Piece Band</li><li>Two-Piece Band</li></ul></p>
<p><p>One-Piece Band heaters are constructed using a continuous heating element which is swaged into a metal sheath. They provide uniform heating around the circumference of the object being heated. Two-Piece Band heaters consist of two separate pieces of ceramic insulation, allowing for easier installation and removal. They are designed for applications where frequent maintenance or replacement is needed. Both types of band heaters are commonly used in industrial settings to heat cylindrical or flat surfaces such as barrels, pipes, or molds.</p></p>
