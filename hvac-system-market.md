<p><p>The HVAC system market includes various heating solutions: heat pumps, furnaces, boilers, and unitary heaters. Heat pumps transfer heat, providing both heating and cooling, making them energy-efficient. Furnaces burn fuel to produce warm air, commonly used in residential settings. Boilers heat water or steam for radiators and are ideal for larger spaces. Unitary heaters are self-contained units that provide localized heating and cooling. Each system serves distinct needs, targeting diverse segments in the residential and commercial sectors.</p></p>
