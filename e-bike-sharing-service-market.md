<p><p>Docked e-bikes refer to e-bikes that are parked and locked in designated docking stations. Users can rent these bikes from a specific location and return them to the same location after use. Dockless e-bikes, on the other hand, are parked and locked anywhere within a designated operating area. Users can locate, unlock and rent these bikes using a mobile app, and leave them at their destination without the need for a docking station. Both types of e-bike sharing services provide convenience and flexibility for users in urban areas.</p></p>
