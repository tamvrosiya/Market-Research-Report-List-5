<p><p>Back pressure turbines are categorized into three main types based on the number of cylinders: single cylinder, double cylinder, and multi-cylinder. Single cylinder turbines feature one cylinder and are suitable for smaller-scale applications with lower steam flow. Double cylinder turbines have two cylinders, offering improved efficiency and power output for medium-sized applications. Multi-cylinder turbines, consisting of several cylinders, are designed for large-scale industrial applications, maximizing efficiency and energy recovery from high steam flows, thus catering to diverse operational needs.</p></p>
