<p><p>Mobile communication antennas can be divided into three main categories based on their directionality: Omni-directional, Directional, and Semi-directional antennas. Omni-directional antennas radiate signals in all directions, providing a 360-degree coverage area. Directional antennas focus their signals in a specific direction, offering increased range and signal strength in that specific direction. Semi-directional antennas have a combination of both features, providing a balance between coverage area and signal strength in a specific direction. Each type of antenna caters to different communication needs and environments.</p></p>
