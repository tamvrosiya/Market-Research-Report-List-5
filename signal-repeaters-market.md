<p><p>Signal repeaters are devices used to amplify and extend the range of signals in various communication systems. There are two main types of signal repeaters: analog and digital. Analog repeaters work by amplifying the entire signal, including noise, while digital repeaters regenerate the signal without amplifying the noise. The market for analog repeaters is mainly focused on older communication systems that still rely on analog signals, while the market for digital repeaters is growing due to the increasing use of digital communication technologies.</p></p>
