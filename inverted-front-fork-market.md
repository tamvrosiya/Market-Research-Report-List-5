<p><p>An Inverted Front Fork is a type of suspension system commonly used in motorcycles and bicycles. Unlike traditional front forks where the upper tubes are inserted into the lower tubes, in an inverted front fork, the lower tubes are inserted into the upper tubes. This design provides increased rigidity, better damping performance, and reduced unsprung weight, leading to improved handling and stability.</p><p>The Inverted Front Fork Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.7% during the forecast period.
