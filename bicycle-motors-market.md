<p><ul><li>Hub Motor</li><li>Crank Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hub motors and crank motors are two types of bicycle motors commonly found in the market. Hub motors are located in the center of the wheel and provide direct power to the wheel when the rider pedals. They are compact and easy to install, making them popular for electric bicycles. On the other hand, crank motors are located near the pedals and provide power through the crankset. This results in a more natural feeling ride and better hill-climbing capabilities. Both types offer different advantages and cater to different preferences of riders.</p></p>
