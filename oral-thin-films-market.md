<p><strong>The Oral Thin Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sublingual Film</li><li>Fast Dissolving Dental/Buccal Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oral thin films market includes various types such as sublingual films and fast dissolving dental/buccal films. Sublingual films are designed to dissolve under the tongue, allowing for rapid absorption of active ingredients directly into the bloodstream, which enhances bioavailability. Fast dissolving dental/buccal films, on the other hand, are placed between the gum and cheek, dissolving quickly to deliver medication locally or systemically. Both types offer convenience, improved taste masking, and are ideal for patients who have difficulty swallowing pills.</p></p>
