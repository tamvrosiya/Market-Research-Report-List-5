<p><p>RFID tags for livestock management come in two main market types: active and passive. </p><p>Active RFID tags have a power source and can transmit signals over longer distances, making them more suitable for tracking animals in large areas. They are typically more expensive but offer real-time tracking capabilities.</p><p>Passive RFID tags do not have a power source and rely on an external reader to wirelessly retrieve and store information. They are generally more cost-effective and are commonly used for tracking animals in close proximity.</p></p>
