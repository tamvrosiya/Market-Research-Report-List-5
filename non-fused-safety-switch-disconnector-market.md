<p><p>The non-fused safety switch disconnector market primarily consists of two types: rotary and knife-blade disconnectors. Rotary disconnectors feature a rotating handle to switch the power on and off, providing a compact solution suitable for various applications. Knife-blade disconnectors, on the other hand, utilize a sliding blade mechanism to disconnect electrical circuits, offering a more straightforward design for high-current applications. Both types ensure safe isolation of electrical circuits, enhancing operational safety in industrial and commercial settings.</p></p>
