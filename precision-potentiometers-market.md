<p><p>The precision potentiometer market is divided into two main types: multi-turn and single-turn precision potentiometers. Multi-turn precision potentiometers allow for a greater range of adjustments, enabling fine-tuning over several rotations, making them ideal for applications requiring high accuracy. In contrast, single-turn precision potentiometers offer a simpler, more straightforward operation within one rotation, suitable for applications that do not demand extensive adjustment. Both types are essential in various industries for precise voltage and resistance control.</p></p>
