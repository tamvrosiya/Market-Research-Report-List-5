<p><ul><li>Low Denatured</li><li>High Denatured</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Defatted soya flour is categorized into low denatured and high denatured types based on the processing methods and protein structure. Low denatured soya flour retains more of the native protein structure, making it ideal for applications requiring functional properties like emulsification and gelation. High denatured soya flour, on the other hand, undergoes more extensive heat treatment, which enhances digestibility and reduces allergenic potential, making it suitable for protein supplements and baked goods. Each type caters to specific dietary and culinary needs.</p></p>
