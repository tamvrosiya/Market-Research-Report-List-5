<p><p>Pressure sensitive adhesive films are available in various types such as acrylic, rubber, silicone, EVA, and others. Acrylic adhesive films are known for their high clarity and UV resistance. Rubber adhesive films offer high initial tack and adhesion to a variety of substrates. Silicone adhesive films are ideal for high temperature applications. EVA adhesive films provide flexibility and softness. Other types of adhesive films offer unique properties such as high chemical resistance or low outgassing. Each type caters to specific application requirements in the market.</p></p>
