<p><p>Active Tags in the wireless sensor tags market are battery-powered devices that actively transmit data to a central hub or receiver. These tags are commonly used for real-time monitoring of assets or environments. </p><p>Passive Tags, on the other hand, are radio frequency identification (RFID) tags that do not have a power source of their own and rely on an external reader to gather and transmit data. These tags are typically used for inventory tracking and supply chain management. Both types of tags play important roles in the growing IoT market.</p></p>
