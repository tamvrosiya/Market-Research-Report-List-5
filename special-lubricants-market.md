<p><p>Special lubricants market includes various types such as lubricating greases, oils, pastes, anti-friction coatings, and others. Lubricating greases are semi-solid lubricants used in areas where conventional oils cannot be used effectively. Oils are liquid lubricants used in a wide range of applications. Pastes are thick lubricants containing solid lubricants such as molybdenum disulfide or graphite. Anti-friction coatings are used to reduce friction and wear on surfaces. Other types of special lubricants include dry lubricants, solid film lubricants, and synthetic lubricants.</p></p>
