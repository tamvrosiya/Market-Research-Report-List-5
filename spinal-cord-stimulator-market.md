<p><p>The spinal cord stimulator market includes two main types: full embedding stimulators and semi-embedding stimulators. Full embedding stimulators are completely implanted under the skin, providing continuous electrical stimulation to manage chronic pain. Semi-embedding stimulators are partially implanted and have external components, allowing for adjustments and programming outside the body. Both types offer effective pain management solutions for patients suffering from various conditions such as neuropathic pain, failed back surgery syndrome, and complex regional pain syndrome.</p></p>
