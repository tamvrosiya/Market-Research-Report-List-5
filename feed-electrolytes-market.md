<p><p>Feed Electrolytes market can be categorized into two types: Micro Minerals and Macro Minerals. Micro Minerals include essential minerals such as iron, zinc, copper, and selenium, which are required in small quantities for proper animal health and growth. On the other hand, Macro Minerals like calcium, phosphorus, magnesium, and potassium are needed in larger amounts to maintain bone strength and muscle function in animals. Both types of electrolytes play a crucial role in maintaining the overall health and well-being of livestock and poultry.</p></p>
