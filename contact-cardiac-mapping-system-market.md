<p><p>The Contact Cardiac Mapping System market encompasses various types of mapping techniques. Electroanatomical mapping utilizes 3D imaging to create detailed heart maps, improving visualization during procedures. Basket catheter mapping employs specialized catheters with multiple electrodes to capture data from atrial structures simultaneously, enhancing accuracy. Traditional endocardial catheter mapping involves standard catheters to record electrical activity, often providing foundational data but with limited spatial resolution. These methods cater to different clinical needs, advancing cardiac intervention precision and outcomes.</p></p>
