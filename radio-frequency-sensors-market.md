<p><strong>The Radio Frequency Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive</li><li>Active</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radio frequency sensors are categorized into two main types: passive and active. Passive RF sensors detect and respond to incoming signals without emitting their own, relying on external sources for power and functionality. They are commonly used in applications such as environmental monitoring and security systems. In contrast, active RF sensors generate and transmit their own signals to measure and analyze various parameters, making them suitable for applications like distance measurement and data transmission in smart devices. These distinctions influence their deployment and effectiveness across different industries.</p></p>
