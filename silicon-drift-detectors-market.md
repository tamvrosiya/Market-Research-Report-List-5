<p><ul><li>BasedonConcentricRings</li><li>BasedonDropletRings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicon Drift Detectors (SDDs) can be classified into two types based on their design: concentric rings and droplet rings. In concentric ring SDDs, the electrodes are arranged in circular layers around the detector, allowing for efficient charge collection. On the other hand, droplet ring SDDs have a droplet electrode design where charge carriers are collected near the center of the detector. Both types offer distinct advantages in terms of sensitivity, resolution, and overall performance in various applications within the market.</p></p>
