<p><p>Active wheelchair market can be segmented into two main types: manual wheelchairs and power wheelchairs. On the other hand, power wheelchairs are motorized, making them easier to operate for individuals with limited mobility or strength. They offer greater independence and are suitable for those who need extra assistance in moving around. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of users in the active wheelchair market.</p></p>
