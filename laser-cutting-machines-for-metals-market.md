CO2 laser cutting machines are widely used for cutting non-metal materials such as wood and plastic, while fiber laser cutting machines are known for their high precision and speed when cutting metals. YAG cutting machines, on the other hand, are suitable for cutting thicker metals and are often used in industries requiring deep penetration cuts. Each type of machine has its own unique features and applications in the metal cutting market.</p></p>
