<p><p>The safety fistula needles market primarily comprises standard fistula needles and AV (arteriovenous) fistula needles. Standard fistula needles are designed for general use in dialysis, featuring safety mechanisms to reduce the risk of needle-stick injuries. AV fistula needles are specifically crafted for accessing arteriovenous fistulas in patients undergoing hemodialysis, allowing for efficient blood flow. Both types prioritize patient and clinical staff safety, enhancing procedural efficiency while minimizing complications associated with dialysis treatments.</p></p>
