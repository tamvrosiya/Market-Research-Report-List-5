<p><strong>The Split Case Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Type</li><li>Vertical Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Split case pumps are commonly categorized into two types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal split case pumps are typically used in applications where floor space is limited, as they have a compact design. They are also preferred for high-flow, low-head applications. On the other hand, vertical split case pumps are best suited for applications where the pump needs to be installed below the water level. They are often used in vertical pumping stations or applications with limited floor space. Both types of split case pumps offer reliable and efficient performance.</p></p>
