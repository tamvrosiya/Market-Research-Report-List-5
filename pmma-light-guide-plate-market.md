<p><strong>The PMMA Light Guide Plate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incident Light from both Sides</li><li>Incident Light from One Side</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PMMA Light Guide Plate market features two primary types based on the direction of incident light. The "Incident Light from both Sides" type allows light to enter from both the front and rear surfaces, enhancing illumination and creating uniform brightness, ideal for backlighting applications. In contrast, the "Incident Light from One Side" type channels light entering from a single edge, which is suited for designs requiring space efficiency and focused light output, commonly used in displays and interior lighting.</p></p>
