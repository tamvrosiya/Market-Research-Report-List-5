<p><p>The medical gas fittings market includes various types of fittings such as connectors, pipe plugs, adapters, couplers, and hoses. Connectors are used to join different parts of medical gas equipment together, while pipe plugs seal off or close the end of a pipe. Adapters allow for connection between devices with different sizes or types of fittings. Couplers are used to join two pieces of medical gas equipment together, and hoses are flexible tubes that transport gases from one device to another.</p></p>
