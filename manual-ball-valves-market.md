<p><p>A manual ball valve is a type of valve that uses a rotating ball to control the flow of a fluid through a pipeline. The ball has a hole or port through the center that allows the fluid to pass through when the valve is open, and blocks the flow when the valve is closed. Manual ball valves are commonly used in a variety of industries including oil and gas, water and wastewater, chemical, and power generation.</p><p>The global manual ball valves market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period.
