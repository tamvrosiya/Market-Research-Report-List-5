<p><p>Software Defined Radio (SDR) utilizes various hardware components such as Field Programmable Gate Arrays (FPGA), Digital Signal Processors (DSP), General Purpose Processors (GPP), Programmable System on Chips (PSOC), amplifiers, specialized software, and other components for communication purposes. FPGA and DSP are commonly used for signal processing, while GPP handles general computation tasks. PSOC combines the functions of various components into a single chip. Amplifiers boost signal strength, and software allows for flexibility and adaptability in radio communication. Other components aid in optimizing performance and functionality.</p></p>
