<p><p>Field Effect Transistor (FET) market consists of two main types: Junction Field Effect Transistor (JFET) and Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field Effect Transistor (MOSFET). JFETs are voltage-controlled devices that use a reverse-biased pn-junction to control the current flow, while MOSFETs use a metal oxide layer to insulate the gate from the channel. JFETs are mostly used in high-frequency applications, while MOSFETs are preferred for high-power and digital circuit applications due to their superior performance and efficiency. Both types play a crucial role in the electronics industry.</p></p>
