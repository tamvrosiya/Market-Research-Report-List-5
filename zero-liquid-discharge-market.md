<p><ul><li>Conventional ZLD System</li><li>Hybrid ZLD System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD) systems help industries meet environmental regulations by eliminating liquid waste. Conventional ZLD systems use multiple treatment processes like evaporation and crystallization to achieve complete water reuse. Hybrid ZLD systems combine traditional treatment methods with newer technologies like forward osmosis and membrane distillation for more efficient and cost-effective water management. Both types of ZLD systems are in demand across industries such as power generation, chemical manufacturing, and wastewater treatment due to increasing water scarcity and stricter environmental regulations.</p></p>
