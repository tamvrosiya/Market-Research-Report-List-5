<p><p>Aramid fibers are primarily divided into three types: para-aramid, meta-aramid, and others. Para-aramid fibers are known for their high strength and heat resistance, making them ideal for applications such as body armor and aerospace components. Meta-aramid fibers, on the other hand, are valued for their flame resistance and are commonly used in protective clothing. The "others" category typically includes specialty aramid fibers with unique properties tailored for specific applications in industries such as automotive, construction, and electronics.</p></p>
