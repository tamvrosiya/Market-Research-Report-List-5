<p><p>ITC Hearing Aids market includes Digital Signal Processing (DSP) Hearing Aids and Simulated Signal Processing Hearing Aids. DSP Hearing Aids use advanced technology to process sound digitally for improved clarity and customization. These devices offer features like noise reduction and feedback cancellation. On the other hand, Simulated Signal Processing Hearing Aids mimic the natural processing of sound through analog circuitry, providing a more traditional hearing aid experience. Both types cater to different preferences and hearing needs in the market.</p></p>
