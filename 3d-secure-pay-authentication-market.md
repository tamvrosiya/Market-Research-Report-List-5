<p><ul><li>Access Control Server</li><li>Merchant Plug-in</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D Secure Pay Authentication market consists of two primary types: Access Control Server (ACS) and Merchant Plug-in (MPI). The ACS acts as a security layer for issuing banks, verifying cardholder identities during transactions by prompting for additional authentication, enhancing fraud prevention. Meanwhile, the MPI integrates with the merchant's checkout process, facilitating secure communication between the merchant and the ACS, ensuring smooth transaction flow while maintaining security standards. Together, they bolster the online payment ecosystem against fraud.</p></p>
