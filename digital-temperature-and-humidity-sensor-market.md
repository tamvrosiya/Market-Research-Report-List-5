<p><p>The Digital Temperature and Humidity Sensor market is divided into two main types: Analog Output and Digital Output. Analog Output sensors provide a continuous output signal, typically in the form of a voltage or current, proportional to the temperature and humidity levels. On the other hand, Digital Output sensors provide a discrete output signal in the form of binary data, making them easier to interface with microcontrollers and other digital devices. Both types of sensors have their own set of advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
