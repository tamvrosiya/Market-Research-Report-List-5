<p><p>Harrow markets comprise various types essential for soil preparation and cultivation. Spring harrows feature flexible tines for light tillage, while roller harrows utilize weight to compact soil and smooth surfaces. Chain harrows use linked steel chains for effective soil aeration and debris leveling. Disc harrows employ rotating discs for more aggressive tillage, breaking up hard soil. Other specialized harrow types include power harrows and offset harrows, each tailored for specific agricultural needs, enhancing soil structure and crop establishment.</p></p>
