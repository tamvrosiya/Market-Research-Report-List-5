<p><p>The dehumidifier market includes various types designed for specific applications. Cooling dehumidifiers utilize refrigeration to remove moisture, making them ideal for residential settings. Rotary dehumidifiers use a desiccant wheel to provide efficient moisture control in larger spaces. Pipeline dehumidifiers are integrated into HVAC systems, ensuring continuous humidity management in commercial environments. Electroosmosis dehumidifiers utilize electric fields to draw moisture from materials, often used in preservation and restoration projects. Each type addresses unique humidity control needs across different industries.</p></p>
