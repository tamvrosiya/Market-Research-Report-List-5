<p><p>TCO film devices are used in a variety of markets, with the two main types being PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) and RPD (Roll-to-Roll Printing Deposition). PVD technology involves depositing thin films onto substrates using physical processes such as vaporization and condensation. This method is commonly used in industries requiring high-quality films like electronics and optics. On the other hand, RPD technology involves continuously depositing films onto flexible substrates in high-speed production processes, making it ideal for industries like packaging and flexible electronics.</p></p>
