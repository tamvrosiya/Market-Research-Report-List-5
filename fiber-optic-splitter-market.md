<p><strong>The Fiber Optic Splitter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fused Biconic Tapered Splitters</li><li>Planar Lightwave Circuit Splitters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fused Biconic Tapered Splitters are created by heating and stretching a fiber to divide the light evenly between two fibers. This type of splitter is cost-effective and ideal for smaller network deployments. Planar Lightwave Circuit Splitters are created using a silica-based waveguide chip to split the light signal. This type of splitter is compact, durable, and offers high performance for larger network deployments. Both types of splitters play a crucial role in distributing optical signals efficiently in fiber optic networks.</p></p>
