<p><p>Ecommerce platforms can be categorized into two main types: Cloud-Based and On-Premise. Cloud-Based platforms are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, providing flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, On-Premise platforms are installed and operated on the company's own servers, offering more control over data security and customization. Both types have their own advantages and disadvantages, and the choice between them depends on factors such as budget, technical expertise, and business requirements.</p></p>
