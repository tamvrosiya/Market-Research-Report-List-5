<p><p>Synthetic Thermoplastic Elastomers (TPE) market consists of several types such as Styrenic Block Copolymers (SBC), Thermoplastic Polyurethanes (TPU), Thermoplastic Vulcanizates (TPV), Thermoplastic Copolyester (TPC), among others. SBCs offer good flexibility, adhesion, and impact resistance. TPU provides excellent abrasion resistance and elasticity. TPV combines the properties of rubber with the processability of thermoplastics. TPVC offers high temperature resistance and good electrical insulation. Each type caters to specific applications and industries within the overall TPE market.</p></p>
