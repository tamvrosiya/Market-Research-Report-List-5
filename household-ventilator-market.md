<p><p>The Household Ventilator Market is primarily categorized into three types- Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP), Auto Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (Auto-CPAP), and Other market variants. CPAP devices deliver a constant pressure to keep the airways open during sleep, while Auto-CPAP devices adjust pressure levels based on the individual's breathing patterns. Other market variants may include bilevel positive airway pressure (BiPAP) machines or portable ventilators. These devices are used to treat sleep apnea and other respiratory conditions at home, providing continuous airflow to support breathing.</p></p>
