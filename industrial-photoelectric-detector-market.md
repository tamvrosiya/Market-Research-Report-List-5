<p><p>The industrial photoelectric detector market includes various types of detectors, such as photodiodes, phototransistors, and others. Photodiodes are semiconductor devices that convert light into electrical current, while phototransistors amplify the current produced by light. Other types of detectors may include photovoltaic cells, photomultiplier tubes, and light sensors. These detectors are commonly used in industrial automation, security systems, and robotics for detecting objects, measuring distances, and sorting materials based on their reflective properties.</p></p>
