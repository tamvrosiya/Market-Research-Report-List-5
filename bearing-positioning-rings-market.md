<p><p>Bearing Positioning Rings are used to position bearings accurately on a shaft. There are three main types in the market - AN Rings, BN Rings, and ANL Rings. AN Rings provide a concentric positioning surface for the inner ring of the bearing, BN Rings provide a non-concentric positioning surface for the bearing outer ring, and ANL Rings provide a concentric positioning surface for the bearing outer ring. Each type caters to specific positioning requirements in various industrial applications.</p></p>
