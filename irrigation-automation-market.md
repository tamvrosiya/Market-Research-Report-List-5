<p><p>Irrigation Automation Market can be classified into two main types: Fully Automatic and Semiautomatic. Fully Automatic systems utilize sensors and controllers to automatically water plants based on specific criteria such as soil moisture levels, weather conditions, and plant type. Both types of systems offer advantages in terms of water efficiency, convenience, and overall plant health.</p></p>
