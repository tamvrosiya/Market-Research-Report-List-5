<p><p>X-Ray Inspection Systems for packaged products come in two main market types - Digital Radiography (DR) Type and Computed Tomography (CT) Type. DR systems use digital detectors to capture X-ray images of the package contents, providing high-resolution images for inspection. CT systems use rotating X-ray sources and detectors to create 3D images of the package contents, allowing for detailed analysis of the internal structure. Both types of systems are used in various industries to ensure product quality and safety during the packaging process.</p></p>
