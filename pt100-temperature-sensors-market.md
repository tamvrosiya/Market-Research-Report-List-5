<p><p>Pt100 temperature sensors are resistance temperature detectors (RTDs) that utilize platinum for temperature measurement. The “Pt” in Pt100 denotes platinum, while “100” indicates a resistance of 100 ohms at 0°C. Known for their accuracy, stability, and linearity, Pt100 sensors operate effectively within a wide temperature range and are widely used in industrial applications, HVAC systems, and food processing.</p><p>The Pt100 temperature sensors market is poised for significant growth, with an expected CAGR of 12.50% during the forecast period. <p><p>Pt100 temperature sensors are widely used in industrial applications for accurate temperature measurement. The market is categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **2-wire Pt100 sensors** are simple and cost-effective but less accurate due to lead resistance. </p><p>2. **3-wire Pt100 sensors** offer improved accuracy by compensating for lead resistance, making them suitable for many applications. </p><p>3. **4-wire Pt100 sensors** provide the highest precision, eliminating lead resistance effects entirely, ideal for critical processes and laboratory settings. Each type serves specific accuracy and application needs.</p></p>
