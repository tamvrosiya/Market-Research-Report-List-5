Regular skim milk powder is processed traditionally, requiring reconstitution in water before consumption, making it suitable for cooking and baking. Instant skim milk powder, on the other hand, is specially treated to dissolve quickly in water without clumping, offering convenience for immediate use in beverages and recipes. Both types serve the nutritional needs of adults, with variations catering to different preferences and usage scenarios.</p></p>
