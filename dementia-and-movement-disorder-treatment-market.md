AChE Inhibitors increase levels of acetylcholine, a neurotransmitter that is decreased in Alzheimer's disease. Glutamate Inhibitors target the neurotransmitter glutamate, which is believed to play a role in neurodegenerative diseases. These medications aim to improve symptoms and slow down the progression of these conditions.</p></p>
