<p><strong>The Color CMOS Image Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Global Shutter</li><li>Rolling Shutter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Color CMOS Image Sensors are used in various applications like smartphones, digital cameras, and automotive cameras. There are two main types of sensors in the market: Global Shutter and Rolling Shutter. Global Shutter captures the entire image at once, eliminating distortion from moving objects. This type is preferred in high-speed applications. Rolling Shutter captures the image line-by-line, which may cause distortion in fast-moving objects. This type is more cost-effective and commonly used in mainstream applications. Both types cater to different market demands based on their unique characteristics.</p></p>
